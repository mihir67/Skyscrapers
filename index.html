Who are we ?

Thanks to our many years of experience in the Polish and Norwegian markets, we are able to offer services of the highest quality in bridge construction. A specialist unit undertakes the planning, construction or maintenance of bridges, overpasses and flyovers and applies the highest quality standards. Our continuous investment in state-of-the-art plant and equipment makes us a market leader among Polish firms handling all aspects of bridge construction. We have the requisite approvals, qualifications and, above all, experience in producing and erecting wide-span reinforced and pre-stressed concrete bridges.
